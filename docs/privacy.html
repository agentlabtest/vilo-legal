What We Collect

  • Account email: used for authentication through Supabase magic link login.

Task Data

  • Your tasks are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (via Zustand persist).
  • Tasks are not synced to a remote database by Vilo in the current app build.

Analytics

Vilo does not use analytics tracking in the current app build.

Subscriptions

In-app subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. RevenueCat is used for entitlement and receipt validation.

Data Deletion

  • You can request account deletion by emailing info@bluewindowtech.com.
  • Since tasks are local-only, you can remove task data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.
